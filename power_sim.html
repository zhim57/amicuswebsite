Crew Change- NY harborDetails

Online calculator for crew Changes.

Crew Change - New Yorkclose

The terminals in New York/New Jersey are especially suitable for crew changes. There are two major International airports ; JFK and EWR nad plenty of hotels. each month more than 350 vessels are passing through New York Harbor. transportation and Hotlel stay are comparatively more expensive in New York than most of the ports around the world. distances between the Airpors, Hotels and the ship terminals are big and there is usually heavy traffic and expensive tolls. We are working to provide most efficient and reliable transfer and crew change experience. To reduce the exposure of the operators and shipowners we are offering prepaid service. the owner pays in advance , and there are no hidden fees for the sevices. taxes, tolls and predetermined hotel stay is included. In case that additional hotel nights are needed those have to be paid separately.
